It Ends With Us


Sometimes it is the one who loves you who hurts you the most

A story about past and new love that is simply heart wrenching.This book will make you fall in love, rip your heart out and force you to reconsider your preconceived ideas about abuse. This is the most moving book that I've read in a LONG time. It was absolutely addicting, but so difficult at times. I don't cry very often, but this one had me crying big, fat tears.
