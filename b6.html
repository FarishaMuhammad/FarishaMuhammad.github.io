If I Stay


"Sometimes you make choices in life and sometimes choices make you."

A powerfully emotional novel, If I Stay is a journey through life and death, in the eyes of a young girl who has lost just about everything.
A well-written,honest and magical book that is meaningful and heartwarming but also depressing to read as it is about surviving which taught me to never give up.
