With Malice


It was the perfect trip...until it wasn't.

Two pretty girls, an exotic foreign location, mystery as to why I did it, and the potential for revenge and jealousy to be the cause. It didn't matter what the truth was - what mattered was that it was fun to talk about.Jill would never hurt Simone. But what really happened? Questioning who she can trust and what she's capable of, Jill desperately tries to piece together the events of the past six weeks before she loses her thin hold on her once-perfect life.
