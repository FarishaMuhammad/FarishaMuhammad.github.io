Every Summer After


Six summers to fall in love. One moment to fall apart.
A weekend to get it right.

This is a sweet & summery story about friendship, love, mistakes, and forgiveness.

It's told in alternating time periods - during Percy and Sam's teenage years when they met and fell in love, and then 12 years after the end of high school, when a huge mistake drove them apart.A simple,heartwarming but intrguing read to pass the time.It maybe cliche but sometimes cliche still gets the job done.
